Print QR Code in sales, purchase, inventory, accounts, and mrp reports.

QR codes eliminate the possibility of human error. The occurrence of errors for manually entered data is significantly higher than that of QR codes. A QR code scan is fast and reliable and takes infinitely less time than entering data by hand. This module uses to show QR code in sale order reports, purchase order reports, warehouse reports, accounting reports, and MRP reports. Very useful to make the process faster, Quickly read sale order, purchase order, invoice, bills, delivery order, picking operation, MRP order no by QR Code.

Features

Error free and faster in searching of orders using QR Code.

Just one click install and all reports ready with QR Code.

QR Code printed in Sales reports: Quotation / Order & PRO-FORMA Invoice

QR Code printed in Purchase reports: Purchase Order & Request for Quotation

QR Code printed in Inventory reports: Delivery Slip

QR Code printed in Manufacturing reports: Production Order

QR Code printed in Customer Invoices & Vendor Bills reports: Invoices & Invoices Without Payment

This application fluently work with multiple company.
